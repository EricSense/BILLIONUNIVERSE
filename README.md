# Billion Universe

**Everyone else is building tools for humans to use at scale. Billion Universe builds the universe that tools, agents, and intelligence itself inhabits.**

---

## What this means

Most products optimize the *user*: faster workflows, bigger models, more automation. That is a layer *on top of* the world.

Billion Universe is the **substrate**—the shared environment where:

- **Tools** plug in and interoperate without each tool reinventing context, trust, and state.
- **Agents** have persistent places to act, coordinate, and be held accountable—not just one-off chats.
- **Intelligence** (human or artificial) operates inside **rules, physics, and affordances** we deliberately design, not only whatever each vendor ships.

We are not competing on “another assistant.” We are defining **what exists**, **how it connects**, and **what is true** in the space where that intelligence lives.
